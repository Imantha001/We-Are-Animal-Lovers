WE ARE ANIMAL LOVERS

We Are Animal Lovers is a Animal protection organization located in Sri Lanka.
It is one of the 27 Animal protection organizations in Sri Lanka. Address of We Are Animal Lovers is Kandy, Sri Lanka. We Are Animal Lovers is located in a busy area and we are covering at least 432 places around it on Srilanka.


Our Mission
1. To rescue animals in dire need.
2. To provide quality veterinary care & rehabilitation.
3. Creating a healthier environment for humans and animals.
4. To provide education & adoption programmes.
